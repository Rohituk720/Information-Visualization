Dataset Description:

    The Population Division of the Department of Economic and Social Affairs of the United Nations Secretariat prepared the 2017 Revision of World Population Prospects representing the latest global set of demographic estimates and projections. It displays key demographic indicators for selected periods or dates from 1995 to 2020, for the countries or areas with more than 90,000 inhabitants in 2017. The estimates and projections contained in this visualization cover a 25-year time horizon, which can be subdivided into estimates (1995-2015) and projections (2015-2020).
